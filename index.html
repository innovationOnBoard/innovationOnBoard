Innovation OnBoard

Fostering Entrepreneurship in UBC Applied Science

Innovation OnBoard is a 6-month program filled with entrepreneurship seminars, workshops and a Dragon's Den-styled competition to foster innovation in engineering. This program is jointly hosted by the UBC Faculty of Applied Science, the UBC Department of Chemical & Biological Engineering and e@UBC. UBC students from all faculties and departments are welcomed. Our 2017 program focuses on three main themes in engineering:

Health

Environment

Food
