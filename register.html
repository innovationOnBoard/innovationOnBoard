Competition Eligibility

To participate in the competition, you must meet the following criteria:
  • Registered UBC student: post-graduate, graduate and undergraduate currently enrolled at UBC
  • Recent UBC alumni: within 6 months of graduation as of the registration date
In addition, if you're entering an existing project or startup, it must be a:
  • Non-incorporated venture that have not gone through the e@UBC Lean Launchpad program
